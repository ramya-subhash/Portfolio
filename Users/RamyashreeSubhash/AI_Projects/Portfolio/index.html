Skill.md — Cake Decoration Ideas

A cake decoration skill that provides creative, fondant-free decorating ideas using everyday items like candies, sprinkles, frosting, fruits, nuts, and cookies.

The skill eliminates barriers to cake decorating by offering quick, accessible techniques with difficulty ratings, time estimates, and step-by-step instructions.

Inspired by my 4-year-old daughter's love for baking, this skill makes cake decorating fun and approachable for everyone.
